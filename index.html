The DICOM Wallet

MAQUI'S DICOM wallet was created to give the patient the posibility to view and store all their own medical images in one place. It's a medical image management system that can be integrated into any medical record system and whichs purpose is to facilitate communication between healthcare professionals, minimize information gaps, and restore the user's ability to manage their own medical information.

Features

  • Centralized storage

    In Argentina each health center has independent management of contracted PACS servers and services, leading to various interoperability problems and making the process unnecessarily hard for most patients. With a centralized storage, patients can view all their information in one place.

  • DICOM files

    Diagnostic-quality viewing and the posibility to download your medical images as DICOM files.

  • Access discriminated by permissions

    Patients have the posibility to grant access to their images with different permissions levels and expiration dates so doctors can view their studies when allowed.

MAQUI is an ITBA start-up project

MAQUI started as our final project for the Bioengineering program at ITBA, but thanks to the opportunities offered by the University's entrepreneurial ecosystem, it is now beginning its journey as a business.
